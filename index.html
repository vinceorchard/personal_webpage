I am a second year PhD Candidate at CREST-Ecole Polytechnique. I am working under the supervision of Yves le Yaouanq and Alessandro Riboni.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Behavioural Economics and Political Economy. I ambition to leverage theoretical and empirical - including experimental - methods to study belief formation and communication. I am enthusiastic about applying machine learning tools to study unstrustructured data from communication - such as texts, images and audio recordings.
