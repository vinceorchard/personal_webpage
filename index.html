I am a fourth year PhD Candidate at CREST-École Polytechnique. I am working under the supervision of Yves le Yaouanq and Alessandro Riboni. I am also collaborating with Flavio Calvino at the the OECD's Directorate for Science, Technology and Innovation.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Behavioural Economics, Political Economy and the Economics of AI.
I leverage a broad range of theoretical and empirical methods — including experimental economics and applied econometrics — to study communication and belief formation. My research relies on unconventional data sources , particularly text. I use Artificial Intelligence both as a methodological tool for economic research and as an object of study, investigating how it is transforming economic behavior.
